Scene 1: Idukki

A song to awaken the senses. A song to satisfy the nostalgia. A song to remember the home we've left, but will always return to.
Mistranslations of the precious words my mother and father have given to me happens all too often. To ensure this song can be understood with all its poetry intact, I've corrected the incorrect subtitles. Now the song is accessible across all the languages known to my tongue.
